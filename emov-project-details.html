Project Details

  • Name:Commercial Vehicle Marketplace
  • Author:Codovio(Pvt Ltd)
  • Date:May,2024

Our Commercial Vehicle Trading Platform is built to streamline the process of buying and selling commercial vehicles, engines, and spare parts. Designed for both individuals and businesses, the platform simplifies listing management, search, and communication.

Driving Smarter Vehicle Transactions

Users can post ads, chat securely, and explore listings using advanced filters like price, location, and category. Whether you’re a seller managing inventory or a buyer seeking specific parts, the platform delivers a seamless experience on both web and mobile.

Powered by a robust CodeIgniter backend and a smooth Flutter mobile interface, it ensures performance, scalability, and ease of use—making vehicle trading efficient and accessible for everyone.
